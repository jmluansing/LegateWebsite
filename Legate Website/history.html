The Early Years

In the year 2000, Legate was formally known as "Securi-Tec". Securi Tec specializes in all kinds of automation gate, security systems, intercom integration, structured metal works and roofing. After 6 years, Securi-Tec evolved to our present company "Legate"

A New Beginning

Officially established on 1st May 2006, Legate is founded by Mr. Leslie Foo who saw a changing trend in the daily lives of Singaporean and hence the need to improve their living environment and standards to this evolving trend.

Our Growth

In Legate, you can be assured of leading and innovative products that transform the way people lives in this modern world. Enhancing high standards, safety and style!

Our Innovation

In early 2008, the "Legate Invisible Grille" is born. This product is a fruit of our desire to innovate and transform the way people live in Singapore and probably around Asia.
